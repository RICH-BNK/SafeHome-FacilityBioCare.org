Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about our rodent control and decontamination services in Kenya.

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?
We offer 24/7 emergency service. For urgent rodent infestations or biohazard incidents, our team can arrive within 2–4 hours in Nairobi and major towns, and within 24 hours for remote counties.
Are your pest control methods safe for children and pets?
Yes. We use eco-friendly, low-toxicity products approved by Kenyan health authorities. Our technicians follow strict safety protocols to ensure your family and pets remain safe.
Do you cover all counties in Kenya?
Absolutely! We operate nationwide – from Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret, to every other county. Contact us for a visit anywhere in Kenya.
What does a free inspection include?
Our expert will assess your property for rodent activity, entry points, and sanitation risks. You'll receive a detailed report and a no-obligation quote for any recommended services.
How long does a typical rodent control treatment take?
Most residential treatments are completed within 2–4 hours. Commercial or large facilities may take a full day, depending on the size and severity of the infestation.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes, SafeHome & Facility BioCare is fully licensed by Kenyan regulatory bodies and carries comprehensive liability insurance for your peace of mind.
