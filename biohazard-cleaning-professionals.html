Why Biohazard Cleaning Requires Professionals

March 5, 2026 | 5 min read

Rodent infestations leave behind more than just damage – they leave a trail of biological hazards. Droppings, urine, nesting materials, and even decomposed carcasses can carry dangerous pathogens that ordinary cleaning products cannot eliminate.

The Hidden Dangers of Rodent Biohazards

Rodent waste can transmit hantavirus, leptospirosis, salmonella, and lymphocytic choriomeningitis (LCMV). When droppings dry out, they become airborne – a single sweep with a broom can release virus‑laden dust into your lungs.

Why DIY Cleaning Is Dangerous

Most people assume bleach or disinfectant sprays are enough. The truth is, improper cleaning can spread contamination further. Vacuuming without a HEPA filter blows particles into the air. Touching contaminated surfaces without proper PPE (gloves, masks, hazmat suits) puts you at direct risk.

Our Professional Decontamination Process

  • HEPA Vacuuming: Removes droppings, dust, and nesting materials without aerosolizing pathogens.
  • Hospital-Grade Disinfectants: Applied using fogging equipment to reach cracks and crevices.
  • PPE & Containment: Our team wears full protective gear and isolates the area to prevent cross‑contamination.
  • Odour Neutralisation: Specialised treatments eliminate lingering smells from urine and decay.
  • Certified Waste Disposal: All biohazard materials are bagged, sealed, and disposed of according to Kenyan health regulations.

Don't Risk Your Health

Biohazard cleaning is not a DIY job. Let our certified, insured professionals restore your property to a safe condition. We follow strict protocols from the World Health Organization and Kenyan Ministry of Health.
