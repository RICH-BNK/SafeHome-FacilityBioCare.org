About SafeHome & Facility BioCare

We are a Kenyan-owned, certified bio-safety and pest management company headquartered in Nairobi. With over a year of industry experience, we have successfully completed 100+ projects and earned the trust of 50+ clients nationwide.

Our team combines modern technology, eco-friendly solutions, and rigorous safety standards to deliver long-term protection. We are fully compliant with Kenyan health & safety laws, insured, and available 24/7 for emergencies.

Our mission: Protect health and security of homes, businesses, and public facilities through expert, rapid, and environmentally responsible rodent control and decontamination.
